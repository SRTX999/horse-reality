"**Potato Clan**" refers to the descendants of the foundation horse Seasoned Potatoes in the online game Horse Reality. I started listing these horses as a personal project to keep up the legacy and follow the offsprings. "Gen Zero" refers to Seasoned Potatoes (SP) himself, so his foals are considered "First Generation". **Please note that retired and deceased horses with no offsprings are not listed!**
